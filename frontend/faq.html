Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers regarding shopping logistics, refund rules, and payment gateways.

For deliveries within Dhaka city, orders are processed and delivered within 24 hours. For deliveries outside Dhaka (all other divisions across Bangladesh), it takes approximately 48 to 72 hours via our logistics courier partners.

We provide a 7-day hassle-free return and exchange policy. If the size does not fit or if you change your mind, simply contact our care team and return the unused garment with its tags intact for a replacement or a full refund.

Yes, absolutely. We use SSLCommerz, the leading certified secure payment gateway in Bangladesh. All credit card details, bKash, Nagad, and bank transactions are fully encrypted and verified under bank security layers.

You can modify or cancel your order as long as its tracking status in your dashboard shows 'Processing'. Once it is updated to 'Shipped', the shipment has already left our warehouse and cannot be modified.

Yes, Cash on Delivery is supported nationwide across all divisions and sub-districts in Bangladesh. You can check the package and pay the delivery rider upon receipt.
