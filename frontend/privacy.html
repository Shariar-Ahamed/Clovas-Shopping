Privacy Policy

Learn how we process, protect, and value your personal data under secure standards.

1. Information We Collect

We collect direct shipping and identity information that you provide when creating an account, editing your profile, or checking out purchases. This includes your full name, shipping addresses, telephone numbers, and order histories.

If you sign in using Google Auth, we capture your public email and name as authenticated by Firebase Security Services.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your user profile information is utilized strictly to provide a tailored, premium storefront experience. It allows us to:

  • Reserve warehouse stock levels and process orders correctly.
  • Initiate encrypted checkout sessions via SSLCommerz payment gateways.
  • Send tracking updates and dispatch packages with local logistic couriers.
  • Suggest product reviews and update order timeline status on your user dashboard.

3. Data Security & Storage

All sensitive authentication tokens are managed by Firebase Authentication, ensuring high-grade password encryption and identity verification. Direct customer databases are stored in secure MongoDB clusters with SSL transport encryption.

No credit card or mobile banking numbers are stored on our servers; all transaction payments are securely processed by the SSLCommerz network.

4. Cookies and Web Analytics

We use local storage cookies to remember your shopping cart items, wishlist records, and chosen dark/light theme preferences so that they persist across browser tabs and sessions.
